## what you can Do 
You should Login and Signup for the Client and then you can :
- Deposit Money (add money into the Bank Account)
- Withdraw Money (take money from the Bank Account)
- Check Balance (Chack the Amount of Money in the Bank Account)
- Transfer to another Bank Account (Doesn't need Explain) 
- Get History (Get History of past thnigs have done)
- change Passwords (change the Account's Password)
- Logout (log out from the account)
- Delet account (delete this Account)
